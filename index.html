<h2>About</h2>

<p>Hey y'all! I'm Mikaela, and I love learning how to make things pretty. My background in design started with graphics, then I veered towards illustration, then I was asked to build a website, and my ambitious self thought, "Sure! But I don't want it to look like a generic web builder template." I quickly realized that needed to learn a thing or two about Web Development in order to implement the design I had in my head.</p>

<p>I created this portfolio website as a freeCodeCamp assignment, but I wanted it to feel like a product I could actually use, and not just something thrown together to meet all the parameters so that I could get the Responsive Web Design Certification. With the guidance of freeCodeCamp and The Odin Project, I am learning the ins and outs of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.</p>
